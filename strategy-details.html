About the strategy

A 2-Candle Breakout Strategy for BANKNIFTY Options, crafted for traders looking to capture strong early momentum using simple price action. Operating on a 1-minute timeframe, the strategy is built around the behavior of the market's opening candles and uses a well-defined breakout mechanism.

Key points for this strategy

Days: M, T, W, Th, F    Trading days: M, T, W, Th, F    Order type: Option selling
  • Intraday strategy focused on BANKNIFTY Options using a 1-minute chart.
  • Waits for a specific 2-candle pattern right after market open, exceeding the first two candles.
  • Entry signal triggers only after confirmation on the second 1-minute candle after open.
  • Uses only when there's a confirmed breakout after the second candle closes.
  • Uses a 1-lot reward to risk at the opposite side of the breakout.
  • About risk-taking to use the initial stop into stop loss.
  • Ideal for traders who prefer clear, rule-based, price action-based decision-making.
